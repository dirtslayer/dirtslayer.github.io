Got my new phone today, a motorola g4. So far its perfect.

![Aide web](/images/Screenshot_20170124-212930.png)

In fact I am using it in bed to make this post. 
I am using Aide web, which is $30 per year, which
I dont really need, but I love software.

I just got this phone today and it took only a
minutes to get most of my favorite productivty
apps. 


* AIDE -
	git enabled text editor
* ArtRage -
	painting
* At Bat -
	watch major league baseball
* Coastline -
	a simple notepad with share
* Daily Yoga
* Dark Horse Comics
* Device Manager -
	find other peoples phones with guest login
* Docs
* Folio Pro -
	avoid facebook messenger
